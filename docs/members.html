Get to know the band

Harvey Dale

Harvey, lead singer of the band, has been singing since the age of two. If he's not kicking it with the band, he's probably at Waterloo records.

John Cements

John is the drummer of the group, and is the one who had the idea to start the group. He has no life outside of this band.

Bill Cements

Bill writes all the songs for the band, but sometimes gets some help from his brother John. He also plays the kazoo.
